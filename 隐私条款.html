FlashGo - 隐私条款

最后更新日期：2026年7月15日

感谢您使用 FlashGo（“我们”或“本扩展”）。我们非常重视您的隐私保护。本隐私条款旨在向您说明在您使用 FlashGo 时，我们如何处理您的信息。

1. 我们收集哪些数据？

FlashGo 绝对不会收集、传输、或在云端存储您的任何个人信息。

由于本扩展的自动化特性，您可能会在其中输入包括但不限于以下数据：

  • 目标网页网址 (URL)
  • 页面元素选择器 (CSS Selectors)
  • 执行时间与计划设置
  • 任务名称和相关配置

所有的上述配置与任务数据，均完全且唯一地存储在您当前所用设备的本地浏览器缓存中（即 chrome.storage.local）。

2. 数据的使用方式

扩展程序访问这些数据的唯一目的是为了按照您的设定，自动执行网页打开和点击任务。除此之外，我们不将这些数据用于任何其他目的（如分析、追踪、广告等）。

3. 数据分享与第三方

由于我们不收集您的任何数据，因此我们不可能也绝不会将您的任何信息出售、交易或转移给任何外部第三方机构。

4. 您的数据权限与管理

您可以随时完全控制您的数据：

  • 您可以通过扩展的控制面板自由地查看、编辑和删除所有任务。
  • 如果您卸载本扩展，所有保存在本地的相关数据也会被浏览器自动且永久地清除。

5. 隐私条款的变更

我们可能会不时更新本隐私条款，以反映我们在隐私保护方面的改进或合规要求的变化。任何变更都将在本页面中体现。由于扩展完全在本地运行，条款更新不会追溯或改变您的本地数据存储方式。

6. 联系我们

如果您对本隐私条款有任何疑问、意见或建议，欢迎向我们的开源仓库提交 Issue 予以反馈。

FlashGo - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 15, 2026

Thank you for using FlashGo ("we", "us", or "the Extension"). We take your privacy very seriously. This Privacy Policy is designed to explain how we handle your information when you use FlashGo.

1. What data do we collect?

FlashGo absolutely DOES NOT collect, transmit, or store any of your personal information in the cloud.

Due to the automation features of this extension, you may input data including, but not limited to:

  • Target webpage URLs
  • Page element CSS Selectors
  • Execution times and schedules
  • Task names and related configurations

All the aforementioned configurations and task data are stored completely and exclusively in your device's local browser storage (i.e., chrome.storage.local).

2. How do we use the data?

The Extension accesses this data solely for the purpose of automatically executing webpage opening and clicking tasks according to your settings. We do not use this data for any other purposes (such as analytics, tracking, advertising, etc.).

3. Data Sharing and Third Parties

Since we do not collect any of your data, it is impossible and we will never sell, trade, or transfer any of your information to any outside third parties.

4. Your Data Rights and Management

You have full control over your data at any time:

  • You can freely view, edit, and delete all tasks via the Extension's control panel.
  • If you uninstall this extension, all locally saved related data will be automatically and permanently cleared by the browser.

5. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect improvements in our privacy practices or changes in compliance requirements. Any changes will be reflected on this page. Because the extension runs completely locally, policy updates will not retroactively affect or change how your local data is stored.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions, comments, or suggestions regarding this Privacy Policy, you are welcome to submit an Issue to our open-source repository.
